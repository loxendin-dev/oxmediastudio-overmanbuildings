Ask a Question
What makes metal roofing a good investment?
Metal roofing is extremely durable, lasting 2-3 times longer than traditional asphalt shingles. It's also energy-efficient, fire-resistant, and requires very little maintenance, making it a cost-effective choice over the life of your home.
How long does the 40-year warranty last?
The 40-year warranty covers the paint and finish of the metal panels against peeling, cracking, or fading. It's a promise of long-term performance and protection for your investment.
Will a metal roof be noisy when it rains?
This is a common myth! When installed correctly with proper sheathing and insulation, a metal roof is generally no louder than any other roofing type.
How long does installation typically take?
Most residential metal roofing projects can be completed in just a few days, depending on the size and complexity of the roof. We always aim to work efficiently while maintaining the highest quality standards.
Can you install over my existing shingles?
In many cases, yes. This can save time and money on tear-off costs. However, we always conduct a thorough inspection first to ensure your roof deck is in good condition and that this is a suitable option for your home.
What does '$2.95 per linear foot' mean?
This pricing refers to the cost of the metal panels themselves. A full project quote will also include labor, trim, underlayment, and any other necessary materials for a complete, professional installation. We provide a detailed, all-inclusive quote so there are no surprises.
