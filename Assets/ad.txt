Headline:
Questions We Hear Before Every Job


Q: How does $2.95 per linear foot compare to what shingles would cost me?
A: By the time you factor in the replacement cycles, repair costs, and lifespan difference between shingles and 29 gauge metal with a 40 year warranty, metal roofing costs significantly less over time. The upfront number is competitive. The lifetime number is not even close.


Q: What does the 40 year warranty actually cover?
A: The warranty covers the material performance of your metal roof for 40 years. We walk you through exactly what is covered before you commit to anything so there are no surprises later.


Q: How long does the installation take?
A: Most residential metal roofing jobs are completed in one to two days depending on the size and complexity of the roof. We give you a realistic timeline before we start so you can plan around it.


Q: Can metal roofing go over my existing shingles?
A: In many cases yes. We assess your current roof during the quoting process and tell you exactly what applies to your situation. We do not give a generic answer that does not fit your actual property.


Q: Is there an upcharge for certain colors?
A: No. All 20 plus colors are available at the same price. You pick what works for your home and we order it.


Q: What areas of Arkansas do you serve?
